DODDABETTA PEAK

Doddabetta is the highest mountain in the Nilgiries Hills at 2,637 metres. There is a reservaed forest area around the peak. It is 9 km from Ooty, on the Ooy-Kotagiri road in the Nilfiries District of Tamil Nadu, south India.

Day 01 : we can go by road. It takes 10 minutes to reach. It is a popular tourist attraction with road accesses to the summit. it is a fourth highest peak in South India next to anamudi.
