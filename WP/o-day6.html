EMERLAND LAKE

Emerland Lake is located near Emerald villages in the Nilgiries district in Tamil Nadu. It is located in a region of called the silent valley, about 25 kilometres from the Ooty town.

Day 01 : The lake is an important tourists and the picnic spot in the region. the lake is famous for a variety of fish in the lake and the birds in the locale.




Day 02 : Day 2 It is also notable for the scenic sun rise and sun set views near the lake. The lake is surrounded by tea plantations where visitors can buy tea products.
