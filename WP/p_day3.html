Days Best Of Punjab Tour -
A Refreshing Rendezvous in Himachal


Punjab's position in tourism has improved significantly in the recent past. It has positioned itself as one of the emerging tourist attractions in India offering rich cultural and natural heritage experiences.

Day 01 : Arrive at Amritsar airport and proceed to Dharamshala – formerly known as Bhagsu one of the spiritual dwelling in Himachal Pradesh. Evening /free time for Relaxation. Overnight at Dharamshala.



Day 02 : After breakfast this morning, leave for a half-day tour of Dharamshala visiting The Tibetan Medical centre, Church of St. John, meditation courses and local monasteries. After this, spend the remaining part of the day at your leisre. Return to your hotel for a delicious dinner and comfortable overnight stay.



Day 03 : After breakfast this morning, check out from hotel and drive to Amritsar. On arrival, check-in to your hotel. In the evening, visit Wagha Boarder and Golden Temple. Return to your hotel for the overnight stay.




Day 04 : After an Early Morning breakfast, drive to Delhi and get a dropped at Delhi airport or railway station. End of tour services
