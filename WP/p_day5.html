Days Best Of Punjab Tour -
Iconic Himachal


Punjab's position in tourism has improved significantly in the recent past. It has positioned itself as one of the emerging tourist attractions in India offering rich cultural and natural heritage experiences.

Day 01 : Welcome at Delhi/Chandigarh and proceed to Shimla. On arrival check in the hotel. Evening is free to relax. Overnight at Shimla.



Day 02 : Today explore the city of Shimla. Visit Naldehra. Pass by Naldehra Golf course, visit Kufri – Winter Sports Capital, Indira Bungalow and Himalayan Zoo (subject to operations) Later in the day visit Mall Road to see Scandal Point and Shimla Church. Overnight at Shimla.



Day 03 : Welcome to Manali . Enroute to Manali you visit Pandoh Dam, Beas River, Sutlaj river link project and breath-taking view of kullu valley . Later Evening check in the hotel, Rest of the time free for Leisure .Overnight at Manali




Day 04 : Today you proceed to enjoy spectacular drive towards Rohtang snow point. Free time to play and enjoy in the snow. Evening free for Leisure . Overnight at Manali.




Day 05 : Today visit the ancient temples of Manali like Manu Mandir, Hadimba Temple, Gayatri Mandir, Vashista Kund .In the Evening one can enjoy shopping in Tibetan market of Manali were one can get Precious Stone, Woollens, Wood articles. Overnight at Manali. : Breakfast, Dinner




Day 06 : Today proceed to Chandigarh – the capital of Punjab and Haryana. Evening free for Leisure.Overnight at Chandigarh




Day 07 : After breakfast proceed to Chandigarh/Delhi Airport to board flight /train to your hometown.Holiday Concludes
