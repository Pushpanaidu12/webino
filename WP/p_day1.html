Days Best Of Punjab Tour -
Amritsar Escape


Punjab's position in tourism has improved significantly in the recent past. It has positioned itself as one of the emerging tourist attractions in India offering rich cultural and natural heritage experiences.

Day 01 : Welcome to the holy city of Amritsar. Arrive at Amritsar airport and proceed by your private transfer to your hotel. Late afternoon proceed Wagah border to experience the Indo-Pakistan parade and lowering of the flag ceremony. Later return to your hotel. Overnight at Amritsar. Note : As the seating at Wagah Border is on first come first serve basis please be present at the venue at least 1 hr prior to the ceremony.Please take care of your personal belongings



Day 02 : Day begins by visiting the magnificent Durgiana temple, Jallianwala Bagh, and the divine Golden Temple (Harimandar Sahib). Later visit the Hall Bazar for shopping. Evening is at leisure. Overnight in Amritsar Note : All visitors are required to cover their heads with scarf’s/duppata/handkerchief while entering the Golden Temple.



Day 03 : After breakfast proceed to Amritsar Airport to board flight for your hometown Holiday Concludes. Lets stay in touch on Facebook\email and meet again on another memorable Holiday. See you soon!
