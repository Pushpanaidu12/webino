Days Best Of Goa Tour -
GOAN SUMMER CHILL


Travel to the fun capital of India to make unforgetful memories with family and friends

Day 01 : Arrive at Goa Airport and take a transfer to hotel. On reaching the hotel, check-in, unpack and relax. Spend the evening at leisure and enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel.



Day 02 : Travel to North Goa which include Panji, the capital of Goa. Today we explore Famous churches of Old Goa which reflect the richness of Greco-Roman architectural style. We Visit Dona Paula, Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, and Fort Aguada overlooking the beach. We also enjoy a boat cruise and admire the sunset views.



Day 03 : Travel to South Goa. Today we visit Shri Mangesh Temple in Madgaon, Shri Shanta Durga Temple dedicated to Goddess of peace, Shanteri Enjoy get together with your travel companions in the evening




Day 04 : Day at leisure. If you are an adventure enthusiast, you may choose to indulge in watersports or explore nearby places and beaches. Enjoy a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel




Day 05 : Check-out from the hotel and get transferred to the Goa Airport for your onnward journey with happy memories of Goa
